INDIAN RHINOCREROS

The indian rhinoceros lives primarily in northern India and Nepal. These
massive beasts have some noticeable phisical differences form their
African relatives. Their segmented hide looks like a formidable coat of
natural body armour. It functions a bit like one also. Flexible skin between
the thicker hide "plates" allows them to shift as the rhinoceros moves

Other Endangered Animals:

  • Whooping Crane
  • White Rhino
  • Sumatran Rhino
  • Toucan
