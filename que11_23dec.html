IMPORTANCE OF CLEANLINESS

Cleanliness is not a work which we should do forcefully. It is a good habit and healthy way of our healthy life.
All type of cleanliness is very necessary for our good health whether it is personel cleanliness, surrounding
cleanliness, environment cleanliness, pet animal cleanliness or workplace cleanliness(like school,college,
office,etc). We should all be highly aware about how to maintain cleanliness in our daily lives.


   Factors affecting the environment
